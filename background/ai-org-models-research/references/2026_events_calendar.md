## Notable Patterns

**Conference Clustering:**
- January: Investment-focused (JPM, Davos)
- March: Infrastructure (NVIDIA GTC, HIMSS)
- May-June: Developer platforms (Build, I/O, WWDC)
- September: Enterprise applications (Dreamforce)
- November-December: Cloud + Research (re:Invent, NeurIPS)

**Best Sources for Organizational Innovation:**
1. **CEO interviews at Davos** - Strategic AI priorities
2. **JPM Healthcare** - Pharma partnerships and AI labs
3. **NVIDIA GTC** - Infrastructure partnerships
4. **Dreamforce** - Enterprise AI deployment patterns
5. **HLTH** - Healthcare AI at scale

Major tech companies report Q4 earnings in late January/early February, often with AI strategy updates:
- NVIDIA (late Feb)
- Microsoft (late Jan)
- Alphabet/Google (late Jan)
- Meta (late Jan)
- Amazon (early Feb)
