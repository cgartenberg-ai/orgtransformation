*Consolidated & Categorized for \"High-Signal\" AI Tracking*

**Category A: The \"Cheeky Pint\" Mold (Insider, Intellectual, &
Founder-Led)**

*These are hosted by people actually building or investing at the
highest level. They combine deep philosophy with hard business tactics.*

-   **Cheeky Pint:** (Host: John Collison, Stripe Co-founder). The new
    gold standard for \"insider\" conversations.

-   **Dwarkesh Podcast:** (Host: Dwarkesh Patel). The \"intellectual
    successor\" to Tyler Cowen; obsessive prep and deep technical
    questions.

-   **Conversations with Tyler:** (Host: Tyler Cowen). The original.
    High-speed, high-IQ cultural and economic analysis of AI.

-   **BG2 Pod:** (Hosts: Bill Gurley & Brad Gerstner). Tier-1 VC view on
    the \"AI Industrial Complex\" (Capex, energy, antitrust).

-   **Not Boring:** (Packy McCormick). *Substack.* Optimistic, deep-dive
    business analysis on how AI intersects with \"hard tech\" (energy,
    bio, space).

-   **The Generalist:** (Mario Gabriele). *Substack.* High-production
    value business case studies (e.g., deeply analyzing a single AI
    giant).

**Category B: Enterprise Strategy & \"The New Stack\"**

*Focus on how businesses are buying, deploying, and failing with AI.*

-   **No Priors:** (Elad Gil & Sarah Guo). The best strategic overview
    of the startup vs. incumbent dynamic.

-   **Stratechery:** (Ben Thompson). *Substack.* The essential daily
    read for the business strategy of Big Tech AI (Google vs. OpenAI vs.
    Apple).

-   **One Useful Thing:** (Ethan Mollick). *Substack.* The most
    practical source for *how work is actually changing* inside
    companies right now.

-   **Latent Space:** (Swyx & Alessio Fanelli). The \"AI Engineer\"
    headquarters. Technical, but focused on the application layer.

**Category C: Deep Tech, Science, & \"Atoms\"**

*Focus on Isomorphic Labs-style \"AI for Science\" and physical world
applications.*

-   **The Cognitive Revolution:** (Nathan Labenz). Operational deep
    dives. Best for understanding \"AI agents\" and scientific tools.

-   **Invest Like the Best:** (Patrick O\'Shaughnessy). General
    business, but excellent when he interviews deep tech founders.

-   **Asianometry:** *YouTube/Newsletter.* Deep dives into the supply
    chain (semiconductors, packaging, energy) that powers AI.

-   **Construction Physics:** (Brian Potter). *Substack.* Niche but
    brilliant. Covers how AI impacts the physical world (robotics,
    factories, energy).
