# Type Specimens

Type specimens are the clearest, best-documented examples of each structural model. Use them as reference points when classifying.

## Model 1: Research Lab
**Type Specimen: DeepMind**
- Pure research mission, 5-10 year horizons
- Publications as primary output
- AlphaFold, Gemini contributions
- Structurally separated from Google's product orgs

**Type Specimen: Novo Nordisk**
- Central AI hub (300-person team)
- Spoke deployment to business units
- Hub sets standards, spokes execute
- Dual KPI structure (central + BU)

- Waymo, Verily, Wing as outputs

## Model 6: Unnamed/Informal
**Type Specimen (6a): Bank of America**
- 95% of 213K employees use AI daily
- No formal "AI Lab" branding
- Erica extended to employees
- Enterprise-wide adoption, top-down

**Type Specimen (6b): P&G ChatPG**
- 30,000+ active users
- Central team, informal name
- Rejected pilots, went company-wide
- No formal lab structure

## Model 7: Tiger Teams
**Type Specimen: Samsung C-Lab**
- Employees leave roles for time-limited exploration
- 100+ graduated startups since 2012
- 20% become independent companies
- Clear exit: back to operations or out to venture
